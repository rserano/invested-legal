Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 23, 2026

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit ANY of your personal data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

Introduction

Invested ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect any personal information. Invested is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • No registration or account creation required
  • No email addresses collected
  • No names or personal identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertisements

Local Data Storage

All data generated by the app is stored locally on your device and includes:

  • Portfolio configurations (stock tickers, allocations, budgets)
  • Historical calculation results
  • User preferences (language, display settings)
  • Onboarding completion status

This data is stored using your device's secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android SharedPreferences) and is never transmitted to our servers because we don't have any servers.

Third-Party Services

Invested uses the following third-party service:

  • Yahoo Finance API: We fetch publicly available stock market data (prices, historical data) from Yahoo Finance. This is necessary for the app's core functionality. Yahoo Finance may collect IP addresses and request metadata as part of normal API operations. Please refer to Yahoo's Privacy Policy for their data practices.

We do not share any information with Yahoo Finance beyond what is required to fetch public stock data.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's operating system security
  • We recommend using device encryption and screen lock for additional security
  • If you delete the app, all local data is permanently removed

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used our app, please contact us.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your data on our servers, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Deletion: Simply uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Portability: Data is stored in standard formats on your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
